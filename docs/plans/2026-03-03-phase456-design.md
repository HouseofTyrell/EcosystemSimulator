# Phases 4-6: Balance, Atmosphere, Legacy

## Overview

Three progressive phases that transform the simulator from a functional ecosystem into a cinematic, watchable experience. Each phase builds on the previous: Phase 4 fixes pacing and adds creature lifecycles, Phase 5 layers atmosphere (day/night, weather), Phase 6 adds lineage tracking and evolutionary storytelling.

---

## Phase 4: Balance + Foundation

### Stats Overlay Fix

Remove the auto-fade timer from the overlay. The overlay should always be visible. Users already have per-element toggles in settings to hide what they don't want.

### Adaptive Population Caps

Scale carrying capacity to screen area. 1920x1080 (~2M pixels) is the baseline.

```
scaleFactor = (screenW * screenH) / (1920 * 1080)
maxHerbivores = floor(60 * scaleFactor)
maxPredators = floor(20 * scaleFactor)
maxScavengers = floor(15 * scaleFactor)
```

Creatures stop reproducing when at cap. Caps update on resize. New `maxHerbivores`, `maxPredators`, `maxScavengers` fields on SimConfig.

### Slower Growth

New defaults (sliders still allow tuning):
- Reproduction energy: herb 80 -> 105, pred 100 -> 130, scav 60 -> 80
- Reproduction cooldowns: herb 8 -> 12s, pred 10 -> 15s, scav 6 -> 10s
- Plant growth rate: 0.30 -> 0.20

### Creature Lifecycles

Three visual stages based on age ratio (`age / maxAge`):

- **Baby** (0-15%): 60% base scale, 20% slower movement, cannot reproduce. Slightly brighter tint.
- **Adult** (15-75%): Full scale, full speed. Normal reproduction.
- **Elder** (75-100%): 90% scale, 15% slower movement, tint shifts toward grey, glow dims. Reproduction at 2x energy cost.

No new data structures — age ratio checks in agents.ts (speed multiplier, reproduction gate) and renderer.ts (scale/tint modifiers).

## Phase 5: Atmosphere

### Day/Night Cycle

- Cycle length: 2 per season (8 per year). Configurable `dayNightPeriod` in SimConfig.
- Time of day: `dayPhase = (simTime % period) / period`. 0-0.25 dawn, 0.25-0.5 day, 0.5-0.75 dusk, 0.75-1.0 night.
- Visual: Dark blue-black overlay (`rgba(5, 5, 15, alpha)`) with alpha 0 -> 0.6 via smoothstep. Small white twinkling star dots at night.
- Gameplay: Night reduces all vision to 50%. Predators get +20% speed at night. Herbivores -15% speed at night.
- UI: Time of day in stats panel (Dawn/Day/Dusk/Night).

### Weather System

Three weather types, independent from environmental events. One active at a time or clear.

- **Rain**: Blue streak particles falling. Plant growth +50%. Duration 30-60s, interval 90-180s. Subtle blue background tint.
- **Wind**: Constant drift force on all creatures (~15px/s, random direction that slowly rotates). Plant diffusion biased in wind direction. Duration 45-90s.
- **Fog**: White semi-transparent overlay (alpha 0.15). All vision drops to 40%. Duration 20-40s, less frequent.

Weather transitions: fade in over 3s, fade out over 3s. New `WeatherState` on SimState.

Settings: "Weather" checkbox in Display section. Disabling hides visual effects and removes gameplay modifiers.

## Phase 6: Legacy

### Lineage Tracking

Every creature gets `lineageId` (ID of its original ancestor) and `generation` (increments each reproduction).

- Color coding: `lineageId` hashed to hue offset (+-30 degrees from species base color). Herbivore lineages range blue-green to yellow-green. Predator lineages orange-red to pink-red.
- Data: Two new fields on Agent interface. Cheap.

### Dominant Trait Visualization

- Glow intensity: Lineages with 5+ living members get brighter glow (alpha 0.5). 10+ even brighter (0.7).
- Feed events: "Line #N dominant (X members)" at thresholds. "Line #N ended" on last death.

### Generation Counter

- Stats panel: "Max Gen: N" showing highest generation of any living creature.
- Graph: Optional dashed line showing average generation over time.
- Implementation: `Map<number, number>` for lineage population counts, updated during stats computation.
