# Future Phases Roadmap

Phase 1 (Performance/Scale) is complete. These are the remaining phases to implement.

---

## Phase 2: Simulation Depth

- New creature species or subspecies with distinct niches
- Territory system — creatures defend/prefer regions
- Creature memory — remember food sources, threats, safe areas
- Food chain visualization — show predator-prey relationships on screen
- Deeper trait evolution — more heritable traits, trait trade-offs

## Phase 3: Interactivity

- Click-to-place creatures on the map
- Terrain painting — draw water, mountains, fertile land
- Creature spawning tool — add custom creatures mid-simulation
- Time-lapse mode — fast-forward with keyframe snapshots
- Drag-to-create barriers or corridors

## Phase 4: Quality of Life

- Save/load simulation state (export/import JSON)
- Shareable seeds — URL-encoded seed + config for reproducible runs
- Mobile/touch support — pinch zoom, tap to inspect
- Genealogy tree — visual lineage explorer for selected creatures
- Better onboarding — first-run hints or guided tour

## Phase 5: Visual Polish

- Animated water tiles (ripple/shimmer effect)
- Creature outlines or glow for selected/hovered
- Particle effects — dust trails, eating particles, death poof
- Smoother day/night transitions with sky color gradient
- Weather visual effects — rain drops, fog wisps, wind streaks on creatures
